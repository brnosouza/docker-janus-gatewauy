Plugin Demo: Voice Mail

Demo details

This demo showcases the functionality provided by the VoiceMail plugin. It simply records your voice for ten seconds, and then returns both a downloadable link to the recording (in .opus format) and a new <audio> element that tries to reproduce the recording on the fly.

Press the Start button above to launch the demo.

Click on the button to start recording your message.

After about 10 seconds you'll receive an .opus file with the recorded frames
Recording concluded! Click here to listen to it, or here to download to it.
